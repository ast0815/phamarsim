sources Module¶

Sound sources for Phamarsim

Sound sources must define the method

get_sound_signal(t)

which returns the signal of the source for every point in time t [s]. The unit of the signal is arbitrary and will be translated into a pressure by a speaker. It should be centered at 0 and the amplitude should be around 1.
